Fried Eggs

How to make a perfect fried egg?

Fried eggs are fried only on one side, so that the yolk is liquid and can be spread with bread.
Although their cooking procedure seems simple, there are many people who leave them too raw or overcooked,
so the yolk hardens and there is no turning back.

List of Ingredients

IngredientsQuantity
Eggs1 Unit
Frying oil3 spoon
SaltA pinch
PepperA pinch

Steps

  1. Put 3 tablespoons of oil for frying or 300 gr. butter in the pan and put it over medium heat-
  2. Let the fat melt until it starts to sizzle when you pour a drop of water.
    This trick is used to know if the oil temperature is adequate to cook the fried egg perfectly.
  3. Break the egg gently so that the yolk does not melt, and drop it on the butter or oil.
  4. Lower the heat to a minimum and let the egg cook.
    When it is already white and still visually perceive that it is raw, pour ½ teaspoon of water on the pan
    and cover it with its corresponding lid so that the yolk is cooked and the white becomes firmer.
  5. Remove the fried egg with a slotted spoon below it when you notice that the yolk is thick but in turn liquid inside.
  6. Serve immediately and accompany it with fresh or toasted bread, Cuban-style rice, onion steaks, beans, sausages, and so on.
    and enjoy It!

French fries

Do you fancy fries?

French fries are a common accompaniment to many dishes. However, nutritionists remind us that it is not advisable to consume
fried foods more than three or four times a week to avoid cardiovascular or overweight problems.

List of Ingredients

IngredientsQuantity
Potatoes2-3 Unit
Extra virgin olive oilA spoonful
SaltA pinch

Steps

  1. Put the oven to heat at 210 ° C with heat up and down.
  2. Peel the potatoes and cut them into wedges or canes. If you want to leave them with the skin, wash them well to remove any residue.
  3. Put a bowl with very cold water and add the cut potatoes. Let them stand for 10 minutes to release the starch. After this time, drain the potatoes and dry them well.
  4. Place the potatoes on a tray lined with baking paper, add the olive oil and salt and mix everything well. Spread the potatoes on the tray and bake 210 ° C for 25-30 minutes, until tender. Raise the temperature to 250 ° C and bake for another 5 minutes, until golden brown on the outside.
  5. Remove the potatoes from the oven and serve them hot as a side dish, snack or accompanied by your favorite sauce.
  6. Serve immediately and accompany it with fresh or toasted bread, Cuban-style rice, onion steaks, beans, sausages, and so on.
    and enjoy It!
